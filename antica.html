PROJECTS ABOUT

Antica Gelateria del Corso originated in the early 20th century in Parma, Italy. Known for their smooth, rich flavours skillfully made through a secret ‘mantecazione’ process, Antica has now launched its full range of authentic gelato products in China. As the newest luxury dessert, Antica is positioned to compete with other high-end dessert brands like Grom and Haagen Dazs. H&M created the packaging as well as key visuals for Antica, which were launched to the public in their pop-up store in West Nanjing, Shanghai.

A seal of quality wraps around the all packs, showing the quality and heritage of this brand from Italy. All packs are controlled, but with a dominant product shot on all ranges. There are also touches of handwritten or drawn elements to show the handmade quality.Creating a master brand inspired by the vibrant energy and the authentic heritage of Antica in Parma. We keep the cheery, distinct yellow tone across the portfolio, which pays homage to original brand as well as the vibrancy and positivity of Parma, Italy. H&M developed 32 SKUs ranging five different ranges, from trial cups to pints to Antica’s specialty imported range featuring more bespoke deserts like Coco and Limone halves.
